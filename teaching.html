I started my teaching experience at ENSAV La Cambre and it gave me so much satisfaction that I decided I should multiply these experiences.

I’ve been focussed on teaching beginners the core concepts of the web (Html, css & Javascript), user experience and coding (Ruby on rails).

Principles

Pragmatic

As little theory as needed, straight to the examples.

Inclusive

Encourage women to start tech studies despite social pressure.

Hacking

Students should be encouraged to expirement — practise makes perfect.

Think first

Coding & design are just ways to solve problems and communicate.

Global

Encourage system thinking, seeing each part as a part of a whole.

Individualized

Appropriate custom attention is given then generalized.
